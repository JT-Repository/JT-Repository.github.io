Farm Shop & Restaurant

We pride ourselves in supplying the finest quality produce and sell high welfare beef and lamb, at affordable prices. We supply meat products to some of the most demanding retailers and restauranteurs who recognize that all our beef and lamb is born and fed at Moor Farm and only travels as far as the RSPB Whittlesey Washes to graze for the summer, meaning it is stress free and quality assured.
