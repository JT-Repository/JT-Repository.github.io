Welcome to Oasis Pools & Spas

Swimming Pool & Hot Tub Specialists in Lincolnshire & Cambridgeshire

We are a small yet well established family-run business with over 20 years of experience. We supply and fit swimming pools, hot tubs and spas to suit all lifestyles and budgets. All of our work is guaranteed and finished to very a high standard. We pride ourselves on our high levels of service and are committed to providing excellent customer care.

Our showroom is based within Baytree Garden Centre - which is an added bonus for you, as you won't have to travel very far to buy plants and pots to help complement your new swimming pool or spa. We have a fully stocked display area including working spas, infra-red saunas and gazebos - to name but a few.

We also stock a wide range of swimming pool and spa chemicals, maintenance equipment and many other accessories. We sell just about anything to do with swimming pools, hot tubs and spas, so if you're looking for something in particular please feel free to call us on 01406 371118. Alternatively, please use our contact form to send us a message.
