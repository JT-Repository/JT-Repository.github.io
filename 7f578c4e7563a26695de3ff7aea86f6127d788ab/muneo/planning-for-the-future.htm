Planning for the future

The popularity of handheld devices such as smartphones, tablets and other mobile devices is on the rise, with more and more people now using their smartphones and tablets to go online.

Recent studies show that just over 50% of UK adults now own a smartphone, and more than 40% are multiple device users; meaning they often start on one device and finish on another. Some believe this may be due to lack of faith in the overall security of smartphoness, others are in the mindset that users switch devices simply because some sites are impossible to read on smaller screen devices; especially when completing lengthy forms. Personally, I see it as that latter and users only switch devices because of a poorly designed or non-responsive site.

This is where we come in...

If you already have a website we can convert it into a mobile optimised or 'responsive' website without drastically changing its look. Alternatively, we can discuss a fresh online strategy and create an entirely new site which will deliver across all devices.
