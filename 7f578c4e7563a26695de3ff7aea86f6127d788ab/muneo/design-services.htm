design services

We design beautifully hand-crafted responsive websites, tailor-made to suit the specific needs of you and your business.

We are dedicated to achieving high standards and use the very latest techniques and cutting-edge technology to design, build and manage our websites. We firmly believe the quality of our websites, and indeed our continued high standard of support, is a true reflection of our passion and workmanship.

We have an experienced project manager on-hand who can guide you through the entire process and answer any questions that you may have.

What's included with your website...

  • Bespoke hand-crafted design
    Using the latest techniques and cutting-edge technology, we will create a tailor-made website specific to the needs of you and your business.
  • Responsive technology
    Incorporating responsive elements into your website will create a better user experience and ultimately broaden your target audience.
  • Website hosting
    Your website will be hosted on our shared servers and comes with the added reassurance of a 99.9% uptime guarantee.
  • Ongoing support and updates
    Keeping your content fresh and up-to-date is hassle-free, as we pride ourselves on our continued high standard of support.

extra services

We offer a range of extra services* which are not included in our standard package. To ensure you get the best value for money we supply these services as and when you require them.

If you already have a website and want to add more functionality, or you have a specific requirement other than those listed below, please contact us today. One of the team will be happy to help.

  • Social integration

  • As well as promoting your business to reach new customers, having the right social media strategy is a great way to connect and engage with existing visitors on your site. We can integrate all of your social media platforms, inter-connecting them between each other and your website.

    Contact us if you would like to integrate your social media directly into your website.

  • Bulk emails

  • With our additional bulk email service, you will be able to contact your existing clients or potential customers en masse. Your messages will be designed to have the same look and feel of your website so that your recipients will instantly recognise your brand.

    If you have important news or promotions that you want to broadcast to a mass audience, please contact us today and let us do the hard work for you.

  • Brand design

  • Your brand - your logo, colour scheme and even the typeface you use - gives your company an identity which your existing clients can recognise and which captures the interest of new clients.

    Contact us if you would like us to redesign your company brand.

  • CMS systems

  • We offer a content management system (CMS) service to our clients, giving you total control over your own website.

    Primarily, this service is for larger clients who have the manpower to administer the website themselves. From our experience, smaller clients with less traffic, and less updates and changes, would not see any benefit from the extra investment. We believe our continued high standard of support is usually more than sufficient for our smaller clients.

    Contact us if you are interested in adding a content management system onto your website.

    * There is a setup charge for this service, in addition to the normal website build fees.

  • Shopping cart

  • If you want to sell products or services directly through your website, we can integrate a simple shopping cart system which will enable you to accept payments via your website.

    Contact us if you are interested in adding a shopping cart system onto your website.

* Please note: Our extra services are available to muneo website customers only.
